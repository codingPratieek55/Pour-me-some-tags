Affiliation Academia International College offers B.SC.CSIT program affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The B.SC.CSIT curriculum is designed by closely following the course practiced in accredited international universities and institutions. The objective of B.SC.CSIT program is to offer intensive courses in the theory, design, programming and application of computers. It is envisaged that a graduate from this program will be equipped with necessary knowledge of computer software system. The B.SC.CSIT program will run through semester system having altogether four semesters in two years time frame with altogether 126 credit hours. Each semester shall have duration of fifteen weeks. Similarly Academia offers B.Sc. (Hons) Engineering, Electro - Mechanical program affiliated with The University of Northampton. B.Sc. (Hons) Engineering, Electro - Mechanical program is introduced in response to a growing demand from industrial and consulting companies for engineering staff members with a wide range of technical knowledge. Not a single college in the nation, other than Academia, offers a baccalaureate degree program combining electrical and mechanical engineering technologies. The first half of the B.Sc. (Hons) Engineering, Electro - Mechanical program will be completed in Nepal and other half in The University of Northampton, U.K.
